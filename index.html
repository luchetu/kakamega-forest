In Kisumu we will Visit National museums for two hours, proceed to KWS Imapla Park and have board ride on the Lake for two and half hours, then have lunch during our board ride excursion on the Lake. From here we will visit the Equator line then back to Nyandela market for those visitors need to buy some presents for their family from community of Nyandela in Kisumu.
